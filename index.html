Built for speed & simplicity

Products, categories, page names, swipe gestures plus the Nobly POS quick search functionality ensure you add the order and complete transactions faster.

Tap Tap Charge

With a checkout flow built to help you burn through the line, Nobly enables you to make more transactions. Keep the orders speeding through with integrated card options & split bill functionality.

Settings, Support & Reports just a tap away

Access your sales reports, saved bills, previous transactions and more in one tap directly from the Nobly POS app. Got a query with your account? Tap support to instantly chat with our support team.

Swipe to add item notes, discounts & more

Need to let the kitchen or bar know a customer's product request? Simply swipe the product to add an item level note, apply a discount, edit the variations and more.

Super speedy table layout

Quickly add or edit tables & set up areas to ensure your staff spend less time tapping & more time serving. Instantly send food & drinks orders directly to the printer, split bills, move tables & more.

Build your brand & reward loyal customers

Set up personalised loyalty schemes so you can spread the love and reward your customers for showing loyalty to your brand. Discounts and special offers mean customers keep on coming back.

Take the hassle out of product changes

Quickly edit or add new products via the Nobly POS BackOffice from any device at any time.Instantly apply changes in-store to ensure your inventory and sales reports are up-to-date, hour by hour.

Comprehensive tracking of your ingredients

Add another level to your stock & cost control by assigning ingredients to your products.Tracking ingredients that are used across a number of products gives you clear usage & inventory reports.

Track your stock at the tap of a button

Full inventory management allows you to easily keep track of your stock levels anytime, anywhere. Spend less time taking stock and more time growing your business.

Intelligent & in-depth reporting
